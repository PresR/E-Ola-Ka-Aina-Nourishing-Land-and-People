E Ola Ka 'Āina: Nourishing Land and People

E Ola Ka ʻĀina empowers Native Hawaiian families by providing sustainable food solutions, connecting them with local farmers, and promoting traditional, nutritious meals for a healthier future.

Mission

Our mission with E Ola Ka ʻĀina is to help Native Hawaiian families gain access to healthier, traditional foods by supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture. We're working to reduce food insecurity while keeping our culture alive through what we grow and eat.
